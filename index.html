Biography

I am currently a senior engineer in Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. Before that, I received the Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Chi-Wing Fu and the B.Eng. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
Now I am fully occupied but also super passionate about 3D problems. 3D Reconstruction, Virtual Reality / Augmented Reality, Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Graphics fall within my research fields of interest.
