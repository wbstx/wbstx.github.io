Biography

I am currently a first year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. Before that, I received the B.Eng. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Virtual Reality / Augmented Reality, Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Graphics fall within my research fields of interest.
